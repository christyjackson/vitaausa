A Pioneer in Education

Times of India, India's leading newspaper daily published the interview of our AVP Ms. Kadhambari S. Viswanathan under the caption 'A Pioneer in Education' in its NIE Newspaper in Education Edition on July 7th 2018 in 'School is Cool'.

VIT has won the 'Best Outstanding Student Chapter Award'

The SOCIETY OF PETROLEUM ENGINEERS (SPE-VIT) has won the 'Best Outstanding Student Chapter Award' in Asia, Europe and Pacific Region.

University of Michigan-Dearborn, USA, and VIT-AP collaborate

The VIT-AP School of Business(VBS) exchange an MoU for 2+2 BBA program in the presence of Chancellor Dr. Daniel Little, University of Michigan-Dearborn (UMD), Chancellor Dr. G. Viswanathan, VIT-AP and Dean Dr. Raju Bala Balakrishnan, College of Business, UM-Dearborn. The exchange happened at UMD-Campus, Detroit, USA on June 21st 2018.

Ms. Kadhambari S. Viswanathan being interviewed in Red FM.

Tamil Nadu Governor Releases VIT Chancellor's Book on MGR

Tamil Nadu Governor Banwarilal Purohit took part in the conclusion of the birth centenary celebrations of former Chief Minister M.G. Ramachandran at VIT on March 9th 2018. He released a book, "Moondrezhuththu Athisayam M.G.R", penned by VIT Chancellor Dr. G.Viswanathan.



About

The VITAA (VIT Alumni Association) network has several National and International chapters, and the network continues to spread.
The International chapters function from U.S.A. (Washington DC, New Jersey & New York, California, Atlanta, Boston, Texas, Chicago, Los Angeles, Arizona, Minnesota, Carolinas, Michigan, Connecticut, Ohio, Missouri and Dallas), U.A.E., Australia (Sydney & Melbourne), Germany, Singapore, Malaysia, Oman (Muscat), Qatar (Doha), Rwanda, UK, Canada, Sweden and Ireland.
