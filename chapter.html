    About

    The VITAA (VIT Alumni Association) network has several National and International chapters, and the network continues to spread.
    The International chapters function from U.S.A. (Washington DC, New Jersey & New York, California, Atlanta, Boston, Texas, Chicago, Los Angeles, Arizona, Minnesota, Carolinas, Michigan, Connecticut, Ohio, Missouri and Dallas), U.A.E., Australia (Sydney & Melbourne), Germany, Singapore, Malaysia, Oman (Muscat), Qatar (Doha), Rwanda, UK, Canada, Sweden and Ireland.
