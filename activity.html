    • CHAPTERS SOCIAL ACTIVITIES


    1. Bhopal Chapter activity (VITAA Training session for rural students on making LED bulbs)
    Bhopal chapter coordinators organized a training session (hands on) on making LED bulbs under the mission of Skill Development India for the benefit of unemployed rural students nearby Bhopal (Sehore and Astha) on 5th March 2017. 35 participants attended the workshop and were benefitted. Two students were placed.
    2. Medical camp organized by Bhopal chapter
    Bhopal Chapter coordinators organized a Medical camp (check-up: oral, skin, eyes and bone by Dr. Chandra Shekhar Amb) to the Phanda Village members, Bhopal on 9th April 2017. Vice President Mr. Sankar Viswanathan inaugurated the medical camp. 486 members attended the camp and benefitted.
    3. Vellore Chapter Go-Green event
    Vellore Chapter coordinators organised a Go-Green event in Superintendent of Police Office,Vellore district on 14th April 2017. Chancellor Dr. G. Viswanathan, Vice Presidents Mr. Sankar Viswanathan & Mr. G.V. Selvam participated in the event, Mr. P. Pakalavan, IPS, Superintendent of Police, Vellore District and Mr. P. Umasankar, the then Additional Deputy Superintendent of Police, Vellore District were also present and planted trees in SP Office campus. Chancellor and Vice Presidents inaugurated and donated a RO treatment plant of capacity 50 litres per hour worth Rs.28,000 and 400 nos. of LED tube lights worth Rs. 1,72,000 (five LED tube lights per police station, for all 75 police stations in Vellore district and 25 LED tube lights in SP Office). VITAA Core Core Committee members, Office bearers and alumni members were also present. Mr. Aslam Sheriff, President, Vellore Chapter initiated and collected a sponsorship of Rs.1,12,000 from our alumni members and Rs.60,000 was supported by VITAA, VIT University. Prof. Swarnalatha, Joint Secretary, VITAA, Mr. Rupesh, Vice President, Vellore Chapter and Mr. Naveen, Secretary, Vellore Chapter helped in collecting the sponsorship from our alumni.
    4. International Workers' Day celebration organised by Bhopal Chapter coordinators
    Bhopal Chapter coordinators organised the International Workers' Day for construction staff members at the VIT Bhopal Campus on 27th April 2017. The following sessions were conducted during the event: Stress Relieve Yoga, Importance of First Aid kit and Basic tips for hygiene. Fun games for the participants were organized by alumni members. First Aid kit was distributed to all the participants. 400 construction staff members participated and benefitted.
    5. UAE Chapter IFTAR offering
    VITAA UAE Chapter coordinators organised Iftar meal to the needy construction workers on the occasion of Ramadan on 16th June 2017 in UAE. Around 150 members were benefitted.
    6. Chennai Chapter - Social activity
    VITAA Chennai Chapter alumni members and volunteers carried out the Creative Artwork Phase I & II at Avvai Kapagam, a school for Special Kids, Chennai on 29th April & 12th May 2018. They all painted the walls to give a makeover to the School enthusiastically and actively. Their bright smiles and faces not just kept the event running but also the kids present there to smile and have fun seeing them paint around.
    7. UAE Chapter Blood Donation Camp
    VITAA UAE Chapter coordinators organized a Blood Donation Camp on 11th May 2018 in Latifa Hospital, Dubai, UAE. Our alumni members in UAE participated in the Blood Donation Camp and donated blood.
    8. Coimbatore Chapter activity - Dental awareness Camp for School students
    VITAA Coimbatore Chapter coordinators had organized a Dental awareness Camp at Krishnasamy Nagar Matriculation School, Ramanathapuram, Coimbatore on 24th July 2018 for the benefits of the students.
    9. VITAA Chennai Chapter activity for School students
    The Office bearers and the coordinators of VITAA-VIT Chapter, VIT Chennai campus and Chennai Chapter in association with Event managers club coordinators in VIT Chennai campus had organised a social activity at Government High school students, Kandigai in Chennai on 28th July, 2018. Students of 6th to 9th Std. participated in various competitions and fun events that were held. A drawing competition and a General knowledge quiz were conducted in tandem to fun games like passing the parcel, pyramid of cups. The school students learned and laughed through the day as it concluded with a prize distribution where all the winners were given mementos and certificates.

    About

    The VITAA (VIT Alumni Association) network has several National and International chapters, and the network continues to spread.
    The International chapters function from U.S.A. (Washington DC, New Jersey & New York, California, Atlanta, Boston, Texas, Chicago, Los Angeles, Arizona, Minnesota, Carolinas, Michigan, Connecticut, Ohio, Missouri and Dallas), U.A.E., Australia (Sydney & Melbourne), Germany, Singapore, Malaysia, Oman (Muscat), Qatar (Doha), Rwanda, UK, Canada, Sweden and Ireland.
